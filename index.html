FAQ

Clarity in every asset move

Find answers to common questions about our asset tracking, compliance, and inventory management solutions for healthcare, logistics, and industry.

How accurate is your asset tracking?

Our system delivers up to 99.8% room-level accuracy and 99.98% identification accuracy, ensuring precise tracking of specimens, instruments, and inventory across all environments.

What technologies do you support?

We integrate RFID, BLE, UHF, WiFi, GPS, and satellite connectivity, providing flexible solutions for both connected and remote operations, with seamless data exchange to enterprise and healthcare systems.

How do you ensure compliance and auditability?

Our platform creates full audit trails, logs every interaction, and generates alerts for anomalies, supporting regulatory compliance and reducing errors, losses, and delays.

Can your system scale with my organization?

Yes, our modular architecture supports deployments from single labs to enterprise-wide rollouts, adapting to your needs as your operations grow.
